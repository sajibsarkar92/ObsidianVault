In the past few years, I have picked up a rather refreshing hobby : photography. I find myself taking candid photos of streets, buildings, roads, nature and other random things. I do not consider myself a professional, but I do enjoy the challenge of capturing a beautiful sunset or an abandoned building. I do not actively look for beautiful shots. Instead, I find myself more content with living in the moment and capturing it as it happens. This hobby forces me to slow down and look at the tiny details of this beautiful world that one might usually ignore. This has surprisingly changed my perspective on life. In this fast-paced world, I now take time to enjoy what life has to offer. For me, photography is a way to preserve memories and feelings. It is like a miniature world where time is frozen. It is incredibly satisfying to look back at a photo and relive the emotions felt in the moment. This simple activity helps be stay creative and objective. It is a peaceful way to document my life and put the memories in a time capsule for my future self.
