About a year ago, I picked up a book called "Atomic Habits". Reading through the pages, I was incredibly fascinated by its philosophy. The book has a simple motto : "Getting one percent better than tomorrow". At first glance, it may seem insignificant, but reading the book made me realize how much I overlooked. In a world where we are obsessed with overnight success and fame, we often miss the most obvious truths. Actual, sustainable progress lies in small successes. Big changes are the results of tiny, consistent actions. I have come to realize that consistency is more important than intensity. When I look back, I can see that my biggest achievements were actually results of hundreds of small choices made over a long period. This realization changed how I look at life. It helps me stay calm and keeps me from getting overwhelmed. I know that as long as one keeps moving forward with small sacrifices, life will reward them.
