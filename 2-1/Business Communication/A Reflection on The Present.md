I find myself thinking about how quickly weeks seem to pass by. It often feels like I am rushing from one task to another without really stopping to breath. I have finally realized that I spent most of my life living in the future, always planning for the next big event or worrying about what comes next. However, I feel like I should change this mindset. I want to be present in "now" than ever. I want to take a moment to enjoy the small things of life. Whether sitting in the balcony and watching sunset or going for a late night walk, I am trying to exist in the moment. It is such a strange feeling to let go of the need to be always productive. I am learning that life is not always about what I achieve, but also how I experience the world. This simple shift in thinking made me feel so much lighter. I do not have to have everything figured out. Maybe I can cut some slack to myself and enjoy life as it is.
